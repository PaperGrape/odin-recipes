Pasta with ricotta


I absolutely love this quick and easy creamy pasta with ricotta and sun-dried tomatoes recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tagliatelle pasta
  • 1 (6 ounce) jar sun-dried tomatoes packed in oil, drained and chopped
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¾ ounce chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • ¾ ounce chopped fresh basil

Steps

  • Step 1:

    Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in tagliatelle and return to a boil; cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, for about 8 minutes. Drain and return to the pot.

  • Step 2:

    Cook sun-dried tomatoes in a large skillet over medium heat, for 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in sour cream, ricotta cheese, and Parmesan cheese, then stir in parsley and basil. Toss in tagliatelle.
